
Overview of noun aberration

The noun aberration has 3 senses (first 1 from tagged texts)
                                     
1. (1) aberrance, aberrancy, aberration, deviance -- (a state or condition markedly different from the norm)
2. aberration -- (a disorder in one's mental state)
3. aberration, distortion, optical aberration -- (an optical phenomenon resulting from the failure of a lens or mirror to produce a good image)
