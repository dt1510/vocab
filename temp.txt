
Overview of noun transgression

The noun transgression has 3 senses (first 1 from tagged texts)
                                  
1. (1) transgression, evildoing -- (the act of transgressing; the violation of a law or a duty or moral principle; "the boy was punished for the transgressions of his father")
2. transgression -- (the spreading of the sea over land as evidenced by the deposition of marine strata over terrestrial strata)
3. transgression -- (the action of going beyond or overstepping some boundary or limit)
